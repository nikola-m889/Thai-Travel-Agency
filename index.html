Overview

The Thailand offers visa-free travel to nationals of up to 64 countries. Visa can be attained in tradional or electronic form. In order to obtain the electronic form of visa, you need to be a citizen of one of the 19 countries:

  • Andorra
  • Bulgaria
  • Bhutan
  • China
  • Cyprus
  • Ethiopia
  • India
  • Kazakhstan
  • Latvia
  • Lithuania
  • Maldives
  • Malta
  • Mauritius
  • Romania
  • San Marino
  • Saudi Arabia
  • Taiwan
  • Ukraine
  • Uzbekistan

Documents required for a Thai Tourist Visa

The following documentation will be necessary to apply properly for Thai tourist visa:

  • Personal informations
  • Passport
  • Credit card for online payment
  • Travel plans
  • Contact informations
